Research

Before beginning to design, one must figure out what needs to be designed.

Once the design challenge had been narrowed down, research began. This included conducting both primary and secondary research, as well as facilitating interviews. This aided in the clarification of the website's audience and goals.

Audience & Goals

Before beginning to design, one must figure out who they are designing for.

With the research gathered and the information gleaned from the interviews, the website's key audience and central goals were determined. This resulted in a clear purpose from which to make all design decisions.
