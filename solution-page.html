Solution

Once one has completed research, design must follow.

Fueled by the research gathered and guided by the defined audience and goals, I was able to create a Style Tile that shows the overall feeling of the redesigned website. The Style Tile includes the typeface pairing and sizes, the color palette, photography, and five adjectives that describe the feeling of the website. Also included are the main banner image with the Call To Action button and a navigation bar with secondary calls to action. I also redesigned the logo and wordmark for the nonprofit and included it in the top left of the Style Tile as it would appear on the redesigned website.
